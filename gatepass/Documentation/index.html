Leave Management System

1. Introduction

With the increase in number of students, better management of leave is required. This helps us in keeping a track of person who are on leave. 'What if you want to go on leave and the the authority is out for a day and you are not getting your leave?' or 'What if the authority wants to see who are out on leave?'
The leave management system is made on PHP and MySQL, manily focusing on requesting leave, allowing admin to make response for the leave, having a track on students who are out on a leave or suspending an ongoing leave.

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this project is to have a track on students who are out on leave, students who want to go on leave can easily apply for the leave, here admin can approve or reject the leave request, etc.

1.2 Intended Audience

Students, Teachers, Staffs, etc.

1.3 Intended Use

1.4 Scope

The LMS provides details of all the students who are registered on the portal, their leave status, their past leave record, etc.
